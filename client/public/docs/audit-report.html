System Overview

WayBank is a concentrated liquidity management protocol built on Uniswap V3 on the Polygon network. The system enables users of any capital level to participate in efficient, automated liquidity provision with yield optimization strategies.

Purpose

  • Democratize access: Allow participation with minimal capital amounts
  • Automate management: Automatic rebalancing and fee collection
  • Optimize yields: Delta Neutral strategy to minimize risks
  • Total transparency: All contracts verified and open source

System Components

  • WayPoolPositionCreator (v5): Original NFT creation system
  • WayBankVault (v6): Automated position management
  • PoolAnalyzer (v6): Pool analysis and APR calculation
  • SwapExecutor (v6): Swap execution for rebalancing

Delta Neutral Strategy

The Delta Neutral strategy is the core of WayBank's model. Its goal is to generate yields from trading fees while minimizing exposure to price changes of the underlying assets.

What is Concentrated Liquidity?

Unlike Uniswap V2 where liquidity is distributed uniformly across the entire price range (0 to infinity), Uniswap V3 allows liquidity providers to concentrate their capital in specific price ranges.

Benefits of Concentrated Liquidity:
  • Capital efficiency up to 4000x higher
  • Higher yields per unit of capital
  • Precise control over exposure range

Concept of Delta

Delta measures the sensitivity of a position's value to changes in the underlying asset's price:

  • Delta = 0: Position doesn't change value when price moves (neutral)
  • Delta > 0: Position gains value if price rises (long)
  • Delta < 0: Position gains value if price falls (short)

How It Works in WayBank

1

Create Position

A liquidity position is created in a narrow range around the current price

2

Monitor

The price is continuously monitored relative to the range

3

Rebalance

When price exits the range, the position is rebalanced

4

Collect Fees

Trading fees accumulate while the position is in range

5

Maintain Neutrality

Frequent rebalancing keeps delta exposure close to zero

Yield System

The primary source of income is trading fees that traders pay when swapping tokens in the pool.

Fee Tiers

Fee Tier Percentage Typical Use Estimated APR*
Low 0.05% Stablecoins, very liquid pairs 5-15%
Medium 0.30% Standard pairs (ETH/USDC) 15-40%
High 1.00% Volatile, exotic pairs 25-100%+

*APRs vary based on trading volume, total liquidity, and market conditions

APR Calculation Formula

APR = (Volume_24h * Fee_Tier * Position_Share * 365) / Position_Value * 100 Where: - Volume_24h = Trading volume in 24 hours - Fee_Tier = 0.0005 (0.05%), 0.003 (0.3%), or 0.01 (1%) - Position_Share = Your_Liquidity / Total_Liquidity_In_Range - Position_Value = Total value of your position in USD

Calculation Example

Variable Value
Pool 24h volume $10,000,000
Fee Tier 0.05% (500)
Your liquidity $1,000
Total liquidity in your range $100,000
Daily fees $10M * 0.0005 * ($1K/$100K) = $0.50/day
APR ($0.50 * 365) / $1,000 * 100 = 18.25%

Fee Distribution Flow

1

Fee Generation

Each time a trader swaps tokens, they pay a fee (0.05%, 0.3% or 1% depending on tier).

2

Accumulation

Fees automatically accumulate in the liquidity provider's NFT position.

3

Collection (v6)

The v6 system automatically collects fees via the collectFees() function.

4

Distribution

Vault commission (1%) is deducted and the rest is transferred to the position owner.

Impermanent Loss (IL)

Impermanent Loss is the difference between holding tokens in a liquidity position vs. simply holding them. It occurs when the relative price of tokens changes after depositing.

Scenario: You deposit $1,000 in an ETH/USDC pool (50/50) Initial Price: 1 ETH = $2,000 - You deposit: 0.25 ETH ($500) + 500 USDC ($500) = $1,000 Final Price: 1 ETH = $4,000 (ETH rises 100%) If you had HELD: - 0.25 ETH = $1,000 - 500 USDC = $500 - Total = $1,500 In the POOL (after arbitrage): - ~0.177 ETH = $708 - ~708 USDC = $708 - Total = $1,416 IMPERMANENT LOSS = $1,500 - $1,416 = $84 (5.6%)

  • You stop generating fees completely
  • Your position becomes 100% in one token
  • You need to rebalance to generate yields again
