1. Executive Summary

1.1 General Overview

WayPool.net is a decentralized liquidity management platform (DeFi) that enables users to create and manage liquidity positions on Uniswap V3, with a hybrid approach that combines:

Non-Custodial Positions

Users maintain full control of their assets through Uniswap V3 NFTs

Custodial Wallets

Alternative option for users who prefer a simplified experience

Automated Yields

Proprietary daily APR distribution system
