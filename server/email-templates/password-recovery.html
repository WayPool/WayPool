Security Tips

Strong Password

Create a strong password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

Unique Password

Don't use the same password that you use on other websites or services.

Protect Your Keys

Never share your password, access codes, or private keys with anyone, even if they claim to be WayBank representatives.
